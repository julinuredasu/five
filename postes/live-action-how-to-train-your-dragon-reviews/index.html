The original “How to Train Your Dragon” film, directed by Dean DeBlois and Chris Sanders, is set in a mythical Viking village where dragons are a part of everyday life. The story follows Hiccup, a young Viking who befriends a dragon, Toothless, and together they change the course of their village’s history.
