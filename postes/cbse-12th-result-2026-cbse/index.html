CBSE 12th Result 2026: What to Expect

The CBSE 12th result 2026 is expected to be announced in the month of May or June, although the exact date has not been confirmed by the board. Importance of CBSE 12th Result in Singapore

In Singapore, the CBSE 12th result is a critical factor in determining a student’s eligibility for admission to top universities. Many universities in Singapore, including the National University of Singapore (NUS) and Nanyang Technological University (NTU), consider the CBSE 12th result as one of the admission criteria. Students who score well in their CBSE 12th examination can also apply for scholarships and grants offered by the Singapore government.
