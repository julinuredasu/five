When will the CBSE 12th 2026 exam take place in Singapore?

The CBSE 12th 2026 exam in Singapore is expected to take place between late February to late March 2026, however, the exact dates will be announced by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) in due course.

How can I stay updated about the CBSE 12th 2026 exam dates in Singapore?

Students can stay updated about the CBSE 12th 2026 exam dates in Singapore by regularly checking the official CBSE website (cbse.gov.in) or the website of the Indian High Commission in Singapore, as well as registering for email updates from their school.

Are the CBSE 12th 2026 exam dates in Singapore the same as in India?

The CBSE 12th 2026 exam dates in Singapore are usually the same as in India, but it is essential to verify the dates with the CBSE or the Indian High Commission in Singapore to confirm, as there may be slight variations.

Where can I find the CBSE 12th 2026 exam schedule and timetable for Singapore?

The CBSE 12th 2026 exam schedule and timetable for Singapore can be found on the official CBSE website (cbse.gov.in) or the website of the school where the student is enrolled, typically a few months before the exams are scheduled to take place.
