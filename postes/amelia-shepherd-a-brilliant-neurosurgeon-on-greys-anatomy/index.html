What Is Amelia Shepherd, a Brilliant Neurosurgeon on Grey's Anatomy?

Amelia Shepherd is a fictional character within the Shondaland universe, introduced initially in the spin-off series Private Practice before becoming a main character on Grey's Anatomy. She is the younger sister of Derek Shepherd, another iconic neurosurgeon from the series, and inherits a similar, if not intensified, drive for surgical excellence. Her professional life is marked by innovative procedures and a relentless pursuit of challenging cases, cementing her reputation as a top-tier neurosurgeon.
